What is a sound bath?

A sound bath is a guided listening experience — you lie down, get comfortable, and let waves of sound from singing bowls, gongs, drums, chimes and voice wash over you. Sessions typically run 60–90 minutes and support deep relaxation, better sleep and nervous-system regulation. No experience needed; you just listen.

New to it? Any event above is a fine first one. Bring a yoga mat, a blanket, and something soft for under your head — most venues suggest arriving 10–15 minutes early to settle in.
